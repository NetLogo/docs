hsb1.0

hsb hue saturation brightness

Reports a RGB list when given three numbers describing an HSB color. Hue, saturation, and brightness are integers in the range 0-360, 0-100, 0-100 respectively. Input values will be clamped to these ranges before converting to RGB. The RGB list contains three integers in the range of 0-255.
